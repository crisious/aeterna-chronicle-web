## Aeterna Chronicle — Solo-Developed Memory-and-Oblivion RPG Coming to Steam in 2026

*308 development tickets. 750+ design documents. 1,248 art assets. One developer.*

---

**Seoul, South Korea — [Month] [Day], 2026** — Indie developer Crisious today announced that **Aeterna Chronicle**, a real-time semi-auto combat RPG exploring themes of memory and forgetting, will launch on Steam in 2026.

Set 212 years after the catastrophic Great Oblivion erased the memories of gods, players assume the role of **Aerian**, the last memory weaver capable of restoring lost memories. The journey spans **10 distinct regions**, from the fog-shrouded ruins of Erebos to the time-warped Temporal Rift, as players seek four divine memory fragments that hold the key to the world's forgotten past.

### Key Features

- **Six Unique Classes**: Ether Knight, Memory Weaver, Shadow Weaver, Memory Breaker, Time Guardian, and Void Wanderer — each with 30+ skills and deep build customization
- **Massive World**: 10 regions, 65 dungeons, 193 monster types, and 8 raid bosses
- **Branching Narrative**: 8 chapters across 4 seasons with 4+1 endings shaped by player choices
- **Multiplayer**: Up to 8-player cooperative raids and competitive PvP ranked seasons
- **Zero Installation**: Play instantly in Chrome, Firefox, or Edge via Unity WebGL
- **Three Languages**: Full localization in Korean, English, and Japanese

### About the Developer

Crisious is a solo indie developer based in Seoul, South Korea. Aeterna Chronicle represents over 16 development phases completed single-handedly — from initial game design through server architecture, client implementation, art production pipeline, and quality assurance.

> *"Solo development isn't a limitation — it's a choice. Every design decision, every line of code, every asset carries my philosophy. Aeterna Chronicle is a story about the value of what has been forgotten."*  
> — **Crisious**

### Availability

Aeterna Chronicle will be available on **Steam** at **$14.99 USD** (¥1,980 JPY / ₩16,500 KRW). A launch discount of 15% will be available during the first two weeks.
