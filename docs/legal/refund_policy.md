# Aeterna Chronicle — Refund Policy / 환불 정책

> Effective Date: March 12, 2026  
> Version: 1.0  
> Payment Provider: Stripe

---

## 1. Overview / 개요

This policy governs refunds for all purchases made within Aeterna Chronicle through the Stripe payment system.

본 정책은 Stripe 결제 시스템을 통한 에테르나 크로니클 내 모든 구매에 대한 환불을 규정합니다.

---

## 2. Eligible for Refund / 환불 가능 항목

### 2.1 Crystal Purchases (크리스탈 구매)

| Condition | Refund | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Within 7 days, **unused** | ✅ Full refund | Automatic via Stripe |
| Within 7 days, **partially used** | ⚠️ Partial refund | Unused portion only |
| After 7 days | ❌ No refund | — |
| Duplicate charge | ✅ Full refund | Contact support |
| Unauthorized purchase | ✅ Full refund | Requires verification |

### 2.2 Season Pass Subscription (시즌패스 구독)

| Condition | Refund | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Within 24 hours, **no rewards claimed** | ✅ Full refund | Automatic cancellation |
| Current period, rewards claimed | ❌ No refund | Cancellation at period end |
| Future billing | ✅ Not charged | Cancel before renewal |

### 2.3 Special Items / Bundles (특별 아이템/번들)

| Condition | Refund | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Within 7 days, **sealed/unopened** | ✅ Full refund | Item must be unopened |
| Opened/used | ❌ No refund | — |
| Technical error (item not received) | ✅ Full refund or re-delivery | Contact support |

---

## 3. Non-Refundable Items / 환불 불가 항목

The following are **non-refundable** under any circumstances:

1. Consumed items (potions, scrolls used in gameplay)
2. Traded or gifted items
3. Items obtained through promotional events or codes (unless defective)
4. Battle Pass rewards already claimed
5. Expired promotional offers

---

## 4. How to Request a Refund / 환불 신청 방법

### Method 1: In-Game Support
1. Open Settings > Support > Submit Ticket
2. Select category: "Payment / Refund"
3. Provide: Order ID, purchase date, reason

### Method 2: Email
- Send to: support@aeterna-chronicle.com
- Include: Account email, Order ID, purchase date, reason

### Processing Time
- **Review**: 1–3 business days
- **Refund execution**: 5–10 business days (varies by payment method)
- Refunds are processed through Stripe to the original payment method

---

## 5. Stripe Dispute Handling / Stripe 분쟁 대응

If a chargeback/dispute is filed through your bank:

1. We will respond to the dispute with transaction evidence via Stripe
2. If the dispute is found valid, a full refund will be issued
3. If the dispute is found invalid, the original charge stands
4. **Fraudulent disputes** may result in account suspension

We encourage users to contact our support team before initiating a bank dispute.

## 6. Korean Consumer Protection / 한국 소비자 보호

전자상거래법에 따른 청약 철회:
- 구매일로부터 **7일 이내** 청약 철회 가능
- 디지털 콘텐츠 사용 개시 후에는 청약 철회 제한 (사전 고지 및 동의 시)
- 미성년자의 법정대리인 동의 없는 구매는 취소 가능
