- Transaction records (amount, date, product) are retained for **5 years** per applicable e-commerce laws.

## 5. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable laws, you have the right to:

1. **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
3. **Erasure**: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
4. **Restriction**: Temporarily halt processing
5. **Portability**: Receive data in a structured, machine-readable format
6. **Objection**: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
7. **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior lawful processing)

Exercise these rights via: In-game Settings > Privacy, or contact privacy@aeterna-chronicle.com.

## 6. Data Retention & Deletion

1. Data is deleted when the retention period expires or the purpose is fulfilled.
2. Electronic files: Permanently erased using irreversible methods
3. Physical records: Shredded or incinerated

## 7. Cookies & Tracking

- We use cookies for session management and authentication.
- Analytics cookies are used only with your consent.
- You can disable cookies via browser settings, but some features may be limited.

## 8. Children's Privacy

- Users must be at least 14 years old (or the minimum age in their jurisdiction).
- We do not knowingly collect data from children under the applicable age.
- If we discover such data, it will be deleted promptly.
- Parental/guardian consent is required where applicable.

## 9. Security Measures

1. **Technical**: Password hashing (bcrypt), SSL/TLS encryption, access controls, rate limiting
2. **Administrative**: Minimized access, regular security training
3. **Physical**: Secured server access, controlled facilities

## 10. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to:
- **AWS Cloud**: Seoul region (primary), global regions as needed
- **Stripe**: United States (payment processing)

Transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where required.
