## 지리적 특성

에테르나 최대 도시국가이자 칼리마르 제국의 수도.
높이 300m에 달하는 **황금 에테르 탑**이 도시 중앙에 솟아 있으며 탑에서 방출되는 에너지가 도시 전체를 밝힌다.
에테르-증기 기관차 철도망, 에테르 비행선, 귀족들의 공중 섬이 공존하는 복합 도시.

### 표면 vs. 이면

| 표면 | 이면 |
|------|------|
| 화려한 에테르 조명 | 기억 소멸 환자가 넘치는 빈민가 |
| 재즈풍 음악 카바레 | 레테 교단의 은밀한 잠식 |
| 비행선이 오가는 하늘 | 지하 연구소의 비윤리적 실험 |

## 비주얼 & 사운드 방향

- **색조**: 황금빛, 에테르 청백색. 빈민가는 회색과 녹슨 갈색
- **조명**: 에테르 탑 발광으로 도시 전체 24시간 밝음. 지하는 암흑
- **사운드**: 에테르 기관차 증기음, 재즈풍 음악, 빈민가의 기침 소리
- **특이 효과**: 황금 에테르 탑 정상에서 아래를 내려다보는 시점 연출 (챕터 5)

### 탐색 BGM (상류층/황궁): 「황금 첨탑 (Golden Spire)」

```
[Audio ACE Step | Music Generation]
Tags: steampunk, fantasy, jazz, orchestral, grand, opulent, dark undertone
Instruments: jazz big band (brass section), strings, piano, upright bass, light percussion, steam whistle textures
BPM: 112
Key: Eb major (with modal shifts to minor)
Mood: grand, opulent surface beauty hiding corruption, Art Deco steampunk glamour
Description:
The main theme for the capital city of the Kalimar Empire — a city of dazzling surface splendor
and hidden rot underneath.
Opens with a bold brass fanfare that immediately softens into a jazz-inflected waltz.
Piano and upright bass carry the melodic line with a sophisticated swing feel.
Occasional steam whistle and mechanical clicking textures remind the listener this is a steampunk world.
The melody is beautiful but has an unsettling harmonic shift every 8 bars — as if something is
slightly wrong behind the glamour.
Upper register strings add shimmer suggesting the golden ether lights.
Loop point: 2:00 seamless
Reference mood: Bioshock Infinite (Columbia themes), Octopath Traveler (merchant city themes)

Negative Tags: dark, slow, sad, nature sounds, tribal, minor key throughout, electronic noise
```

```
[Audio ACE Step | Music Generation]
Tags: steampunk, dark, melancholic, jazz noir, industrial, oppressive
Instruments: muted trumpet, solo clarinet, droning accordion, industrial percussion (metal hits), sparse piano
BPM: 76
Key: C minor
Mood: oppressive, melancholic, social contrast, noir, desperate survival
Description:
The slum district theme — directly contrasting the upper city's glamour.
A muted trumpet plays a tired, falling melody over a droning accordion.

The piano plays fragmented chords — the same melody as the upper city theme, but broken and slowed.
The listener should feel the direct relationship: this is what was sacrificed to build the golden spire above.

```
[Audio ACE Step | Music Generation]
Tags: boss, dark fantasy, industrial, orchestral, cold, mechanical, tragic
Instruments: full orchestra, distorted cello, mechanical/industrial percussion, cold synth, heavy brass
BPM: 124
Key: F minor
Mood: cold, relentless, mechanical grief, the sound of a human becoming a machine
Description:
Battle theme for the memory hunter captain — a man whose grief fossilized into coldness.
Opens with a mechanical, metronomic rhythm — precise, inhuman, relentless.
Heavy brass enters with a march-like theme: the memory hunter unit's motif, martial and cold.
Beneath the precision: a distorted solo cello playing a deeply human, grieving melodic fragment —
the ghost of who he was before the exoskeleton.
The two elements (mechanical + grieving cello) fight for dominance throughout the piece.
In the final phase, the cello briefly overtakes the machines — before being submerged again.
Loop point: 2:15 seamless
